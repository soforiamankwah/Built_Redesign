FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Built. Can't find an answer? Our team is ready to help.

What is small business accounting software?+
Small business accounting software is a digital tool that helps business owners manage their finances — from invoicing and expense tracking to payroll and financial reporting. Built is a simple, cloud-based accounting platform that gives SMEs, startups and growing businesses a complete 360° view of their finances in one place.
How do I calculate taxes on my invoices with Built?+
Built automatically calculates taxes on your invoices based on your business location and tax settings. Stay tax compliant effortlessly, no manual calculations needed.
Do I need accounting software for my small business?+
Yes — if you want to save time, reduce errors and stay on top of your finances, accounting software is essential. Built automates your bookkeeping, tracks your expenses and generates financial reports so you can focus on growing your business instead of managing spreadsheets.
Is my financial data safe on Built?+
Absolutely. Built protects your data with bank-level encryption, 24/7 monitoring and automatic backups. We are registered with the Data Protection Commission — Registration No: DP19100003095B — so your data is always safe, compliant and recoverable.
Can I access Built from my phone or any device?+
Yes, Built is fully cloud-based and accessible from any smartphone, tablet or laptop. Download the Built Accounting app on Google Play Store or the Apple App Store and run your business on the go.
